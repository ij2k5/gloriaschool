    Welcome to the Gloria School Homepage!!

    Gloria School is a small school that started with one class in the first year of 2016, but it is a happy community where all students are studying healthy and happily.


    WE HAVE A DREAM!

    THE FIRST is to educate all students at Gloria School to become wise people. So it is to make them a person who will not succumb to any adversity.

    THE SECOND is to educate all students at Gloria School to be faithful people. So it is to make Bangladesh is full of faithful people.

    THE THIRD is to educate all students at Gloria School to be people who love their neighbors. So it is to make Bangladesh a happy country.

    FINALLY, to educate all students at Gloria School to become competent people. So it is to make Bangladesh a prosperous country in the world.

    To make this dream come true, all of our teachers and staff will effort together as one.

    Please pray together to make this dream come true.
